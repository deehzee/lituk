# What Is the UK

## The countries of the UK
- the **UK** is made up of:
  - **England**
  - **Scotland**
  - **Wales**
  - **Northern Ireland**
- the rest of **Ireland** is an **independent country**

## Official name / key terms
- the full official name is:
  - **the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland**
- **Great Britain** means:
  - **England**
  - **Scotland**
  - **Wales**
- **Great Britain** does **not** include **Northern Ireland**
- in the study guide, terms like **Britain** and **British** are used to refer
  to everyone in the UK

## Places linked to the UK but not part of it
- some islands are closely linked to the UK but are **not part of it**
- these include:
  - the **Channel Islands**
  - the **Isle of Man**
- these have their own governments and are called **Crown dependencies**

### British overseas territories
- there are also **British overseas territories** in other parts of the world
- examples:
  - **St Helena**
  - **Falkland Islands**
- these are linked to the UK but are **not part of the UK**

## Government
- the UK is governed by **Parliament in Westminster**
- **Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland** also have their own:
  - parliaments
  - assemblies
- these have **devolved powers** in certain areas
